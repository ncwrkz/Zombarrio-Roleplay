Rules

Keep it immersive and fair. These are standard RP rules — customize as you like.

Roleplay First

No random killing. Every conflict must have RP reason and escalation.

No Metagaming

Don’t use out-of-character info (Discord/streams) in-game.

No Powergaming

Don’t force actions that remove others’ ability to roleplay.

FearRP

Value your life. Zombies and guns are real threats.

Combat Logging

No disconnecting to avoid consequences.

Respect

No harassment, hate speech, or toxic behavior.

Tip: Put your full updated rules in your Discord and link it here. This page can be the “summary”.
Staff decisions are final. Use Support page for appeals/tickets.
